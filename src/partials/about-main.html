Who I am

I plan and coordinate personalized events with the artistic imagination to the guest’s utter delight and within the client’s reasonable budget. I have been extensively travelling in Italy for over a decade and managed to discover the real gems of this country! I’m always buzzing with energy, enthusiasm and curiosity to get to know more about Italy, its treasures, the best hospitality, catering and other spots it can offer.
